MOTIONSCRIPT GUIDE

  • MOVE X Y Z [F] - Linear move to target TCP coordinate. Speed parameter F overrides max velocity.
  • GRIPPER [OPEN/CLOSE] - Open or close physical claw.
  • TOOL [ON/OFF] - Trigger laser/soldering heat tool.
  • WAIT [ms] - Dwell in place for specified duration.
  • SPEED [vel] [acc] - Sets global velocity (mm/s) and acceleration (mm/s²).
  • HOME - Move back to home calibration vector.
